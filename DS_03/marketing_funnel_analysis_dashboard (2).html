Recommendations

Critical: top-of-funnel drop-off. Only 7.5% of visitors convert to leads — the biggest leak in the funnel. A/B test landing page CTAs, reduce form fields to 3 or fewer, and add exit-intent popups to recapture bouncing traffic.
Scale paid search — highest lead conversion. Paid search converts at 11.2% vs 5.8% organic average. Increasing paid search budget by 30% could yield ~420 additional leads per month at the current CPL of $34.
Improve lead-to-MQL nurture. 70% of leads don't qualify. Implement a lead scoring model (firmographics + behavior) and a 3-email drip sequence within 48 hours of signup to surface high-intent buyers faster.
Double down on referral — lowest CPL at $18. Referral has the best cost efficiency but low volume. Launch a structured referral program with incentives (e.g. 1 month free) to scale this channel from 8% to 20% of total lead mix.
