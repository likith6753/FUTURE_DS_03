# FUTURE_DS_03
# 📊 Marketing & Lead Funnel Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing and lead funnel data to understand how users progress through the customer journey—from **Visitors → Leads → Customers**. The objective is to identify conversion bottlenecks, evaluate marketing channel performance, and provide actionable recommendations that improve conversion rates and overall business growth.

The analysis reflects real-world marketing analytics workflows used by startups, SaaS companies, digital marketing agencies, and sales organizations to optimize acquisition and revenue.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Clean and prepare marketing funnel data for analysis
* Measure traffic-to-lead and lead-to-customer conversion rates
* Identify user drop-off points throughout the funnel
* Compare performance across marketing channels, campaigns, and time periods
* Generate business insights to improve customer acquisition and conversions
